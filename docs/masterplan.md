## 3. CLIENT JOURNEY — STEP BY STEP <a name="client-journey"></a>

### Step 1: INQUIRY
Client expresses interest (DM, link, referral).

**Your response:**
> "Thanks for reaching out! The first step is a short intake form — it takes about 10-12 minutes and helps me understand your business so I can make sure this reset is the right fit. Here's the link: [JOTFORM LINK]"

### Step 2: INTAKE SUBMITTED
Client completes the JotForm intake.

**Your action:**
1. Run the **Quick Scan** prompt (ifprompts.md, Prompt 1) — 5 minutes
2. Determine fit: GREEN / YELLOW / RED

### Step 3: ACCEPTANCE + PAYMENT
If GREEN or YELLOW:

**Your response:**
> "Thanks for completing the intake — this is exactly the kind of business I love helping. Your responses give me a really clear picture, and I'm already seeing some great opportunities for your reset.
>
> Once payment is completed, I'll begin preparing your personalized reset and we'll schedule your 90-minute session.
>
> Payment link: [PAYMENT LINK]
> Scheduling link: [CALENDLY LINK]"

If RED:

**Your response (kind decline):**
> "Thanks so much for taking the time to fill out the intake. After reviewing your responses, I think [honest but gentle reason — e.g., 'your business might benefit more from X before a marketing reset' or 'this service is designed for Y, and your needs might be better served by Z']. I want to make sure you get the right support. [Optional: recommend a resource or alternative]."

### Step 4: AI ANALYSIS + PREP (~45-60 minutes)

**Workflow:**
1. Export intake from JotForm as PDF (or copy responses)
2. Run **Master Analysis** (ifprompts.md, Prompt 2) — 2 min to run, 30-40 min to review/personalize
3. Run **Client Profile Summary** (ifprompts.md, Prompt 4) — 2 min to run, 3 min to review
4. If they wrote a lot in Q47, run **Special Notes Integration** (ifprompts.md, Prompt 5) — 7 min
5. Run **Roadmap Generator** (ncroadmap.md) with Master Analysis output — 3 min to run, 15-20 min to review/personalize
6. Generate their **10 content ideas** using prompts.md generation prompts — 10 min
7. Generate their **weekly system** using wsprompts.md generator — 5 min
8. Review everything. Note your top 3 talking points for the session.

**Time budget:** 45-60 minutes total

### Step 5: 90-MINUTE SESSION
See detailed flow below.

### Step 6: DELIVERABLE SENT
Within 24 hours after the session, send the client their deliverable package.

**The package includes:**
- Their personalized roadmap (from ncroadmap.md generator output)
- Workflow instructions (ncworkflow.md — highlight which 1-2 workflows are their priority)
- Copy-paste prompt sheet (ncworkflowprompts.md)
- Their 10 tailored content ideas (from prompts.md generator output)

**Delivery format:** [TO BE DECIDED — PDF, Google Doc, or Notion page. See handoff.md Section 16.]

## 5. 90-MINUTE SESSION FLOW <a name="session-flow"></a>

### Minute-by-Minute Map

| Time | Block | What Happens | Notes |
|------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 0:00-0:10 | Welcome & Connection | Welcome them. Set expectations. Ask 1-2 clarifying questions from their intake. | Build rapport first. Don't dive into "findings." Make them feel comfortable. |
| 0:10-0:30 | Diagnosis & Attention Leaks | Walk through their business snapshot. Share what's already working (start positive). Present attention leaks as opportunities. | Frame as "here's where you can recapture attention" — never "here's what's wrong." Use the roadmap as your guide. |
| 0:30-0:50 | Weekly System & Client Attraction Plan | Present their customized weekly system. Walk through each action. Show how it fits their time capacity. | This is the "relief" moment. Keep it simple. If they look overwhelmed, simplify further. |
| 0:50-1:10 | AI Workflows & Live Demo | Introduce the 1-2 most relevant workflows. Do a LIVE DEMO: open ChatGPT, paste a prompt with their business details, show the output. | This is the "wow" moment. Live demo builds confidence. Let them see it's not scary. |
| 1:10-1:25 | 90-Day Plan & Week 1 Action Card | Walk through Month 1, 2, 3 briefly. Zoom in on Week 1 Action Card. Make sure they know exactly what to do first. | The Week 1 Card is the most important deliverable. If they leave knowing these 3 actions, the session worked. |
| 1:25-1:30 | Q&A, Next Steps & Close | Answer questions. Confirm deliverable delivery. Mention 30-day check-in. Ask for initial reaction/feedback. | End strong. "You're not starting from zero anymore. You have a system." |

### Session Delivery Tips

**For the Overwhelmed Poster:**
- Acknowledge they've been working hard — the problem isn't effort, it's direction
- Focus on the Content Engine workflow — replace random posting with strategic batching
- Show them how fewer, better posts can outperform daily random content

**For the Avoider:**
- Don't make them feel guilty about not posting
- Lead with the Retention Loop — it barely requires social media
- Show non-social-media options first (texting clients, asking for reviews, referral outreach)
- Only introduce social media if they're open to it

**For clients flagged as WATCH (burnout/low confidence):**
- Move slower through the material
- Check in frequently: "How does this feel? Too much?"
- Be ready to simplify the weekly system on the spot
- Emphasize: "You don't have to do all of this. Start with one thing."

**For clients flagged as REDIRECT:**
- Address the real bottleneck first (the first 5-10 minutes after the diagnosis section)
- Be honest but compassionate: "I want to flag something before we go further..."
- Still deliver the marketing system, but note which pieces to prioritize after the underlying issue is addressed

### Live Demo Script (Minute 50-70)

1. "Let me show you how easy these prompts are to use. I'm going to open [AI tool] right now."
2. Open ChatGPT or Claude on screen share
3. "I'm going to use the Content Brainstorm prompt — CE-1 from your prompt sheet."
4. Paste CE-1, fill in their business details live
5. Hit enter. Let them see the output generate
6. "See? You just got 10 content ideas in about 30 seconds. You pick the ones you like, and then use CE-2 to turn them into posts."
7. "This is yours to keep. Every prompt on your sheet works the same way — copy, paste, fill in the blanks, and go."

## 7. 30-DAY FOLLOW-UP <a name="follow-up"></a>

### Timing
30 days after the session (set a calendar reminder when the session is booked).

### Format
15-minute video call OR voice note exchange (client's preference).

### Structure

**3 Questions to Ask:**
1. "What did you implement from the reset?"
2. "What results have you noticed — even small ones?"
3. "What felt confusing or hard to follow through on?"

**Based on Their Answers:**

| If They Say... | Your Response |
|---------------|---------------|
| "I did everything!" | Celebrate. Identify what's working. Suggest what to optimize next. Ask for testimonial. |
| "I did some of it" | Celebrate what they did. Identify the barrier. Simplify if needed. Encourage. |
| "I haven't done much" | No judgment. Ask what got in the way. Simplify to just 1 action. Restart their Week 1 Card. |
| "I have questions about the prompts" | Walk them through it. Offer to do one quick live demo. |

**After the Check-In:**

1. **If positive experience:** Request a testimonial
   > "I'm so glad the reset has been helpful! Would you be open to sharing a quick testimonial about your experience? Even 2-3 sentences would be incredibly helpful as I grow this service. You can text it, voice note it, or I can send you a quick form."

2. **If positive experience:** Offer referral incentive
   > "If you know anyone who could use a reset like this — a friend, a fellow [service provider], someone in your network — I'd love to offer you [INCENTIVE: $50 off your next service / a free add-on / etc.] as a thank-you for any referral."

3. **Document the outcome** for your records:
   - What they implemented
   - What results they reported
   - Testimonial received? (yes/no)
   - Referral interest? (yes/no)
   - Any notes for improving the service

## 8. POLICIES <a name="policies"></a>

### Cancellation & Refund Policy
> Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before the scheduled session. No refund for no-shows or cancellations within 48 hours.

**Where this should appear:**
- Payment confirmation message
- Scheduling confirmation message
- Intake form expectations section references it implicitly (consent checkbox)

### Scope Boundaries
The reset provides:
- One-time diagnostic + system build
- 90-minute session
- Deliverable package
- 30-day follow-up check-in (15 min)

The reset does NOT provide:
- Ongoing marketing management
- Social media management
- Content creation services
- Unlimited follow-up or coaching
- Guaranteed results

**If a client asks for more:**
> "I'm glad the reset was helpful! What you're describing sounds like it might be a good fit for [ongoing support / a follow-up session / a deeper dive]. Right now I'm focused on the reset format, but let me think about how I might be able to help with that. I'll follow up."

This protects scope while leaving the door open for future offerings.

### Privacy & Data
- Client intake data is used only for their reset
- No client information is shared publicly without explicit permission
- Testimonials require client consent
- AI tools process intake data for analysis — the human (Karli) reviews and personalizes everything

## 11. COMMON SCENARIOS & EDGE CASES <a name="scenarios"></a>

### "I don't use social media at all"
- Lead with Retention Loop (Workflow 3) — texting clients, asking for reviews, referral outreach
- Introduce Google Business as a low-barrier visibility option (it's not really "social media")
- Show them the non-social-media content ideas from prompts.md (7 of 10 work without social media)
- Don't force social media. Some businesses thrive on referrals + reviews alone

### "I'm fully booked — why do I need this?"
- This client needs better clients or higher pricing, not more volume
- Focus on: specialization, pricing strategy, attracting ideal clients, filtering out bad fits
- The weekly system should focus on trust-building and positioning, not visibility
- Their referral system should target quality, not quantity

### "I already use AI"
- Skip the basics. Don't explain what ChatGPT is.
- Focus on the system and strategy, not the tool
- Give them the prompts and let them run — they'll know how to iterate
- Suggest advanced moves: content batching, analytics integration, automated workflows

### "This feels like too much"
- Stop. Simplify. Zoom in on the Week 1 Action Card only.
- "Forget the rest of the document for now. What are your 3 actions this week?"
- Drop to Tier 1 weekly system regardless of what they originally said
- Validate their feeling: "This is a lot to take in during one session. That's why everything is written down — you can come back to it piece by piece."

### "My problem isn't marketing — it's [pricing / burnout / personal stuff]"
- This is a REDIRECT flag from the intake analysis
- Acknowledge it directly: "I want to be honest with you about something I noticed..."
- Recommend addressing the underlying issue first or alongside the marketing system
- Still deliver the reset — but frame it as "this is here when you're ready"
- Consider whether a referral to another resource is appropriate (business coach, therapist, financial counselor)

### "Can you just do it for me?"
- Compassionate but firm boundary: "I understand the desire to hand this off — and I think that's what makes agencies feel so appealing. But what makes this reset different is that YOU own the system. My job is to make it so simple you can run it yourself."
- If they persist, note this for future product development (done-for-you tier)
